My name is Jargal Boldbaatar. I was born on June 13, 2005. I grew up in the countryside, where I spent much of my childhood surrounded by nature, especially during holidays.


2009 - My educational journey began at Kherlen 4th Kindergarten. In 2011, I continued my studies at Kherlen 4th School in Khentii Province.


2018 - I moved to Baganuur, a district of Ulaanbaatar, where I attended Bolovsrol Complex School and completed my high school education.


2023 - I began my undergraduate studies at GMIT University, pursuing a degree in engineering. My interests focus on industrial automation, robotics, and innovation-driven entrepreneurship.

These images represent where I come from. From my school life in Khentii and Baganuur to the countryside, where I was raised in a nomadic family.
